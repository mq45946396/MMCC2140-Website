Tom's Burger Company
Family owned for 50 years

Est 1970

Born in Dee Why in 1970, we've been making quality food for beachgoers for the last 50 years. Started by a team of brothers, Tom's Burger Company has become a hallmark of the area.

Quality food

Made with fresh, tasty and organically sourced meat and vegetables, with staff trained know know our recipes inside and out, know with every bite that you're doing right by yourself and the planet.

Team inspired

Inspired by our brother-pair founders, Tom's Burger Company continues to be run by a dedicated and enthusiastic team, who carry on the same teamwork values which made the food so special to begin with.
